Pizza

Description

A dish made typically of flattened bread dough spread with a savory mixture usually including tomatoes and cheese and often other toppings and baked

Ingredients

  • Warm water
  • Sugar
  • Active dry yeast
  • Flour
  • Virgin olive oil
  • Kosher Salt
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Basil leaf

Steps

  1. Bloom the yeast by sprinkling the sugar and yeast in the warm water. Let it sit for 10 minutes until bubbles form in the surface.
  2. Combine the flour and salt. Add the olive oil and bloomed yeast mixture.
  3. Knead the dough for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Let the dough rise for at least an hour or up to 24 hours.
